Harley Davidson Street 750

The Harley-Davidson Street 750 was India’s first BS6 motorcycle to go on sale way back in August 2019. It's also the most affordable Harley one can buy in India. To keep the ball rolling, the brand has announced a massive discount of Rs 65,000 on the Street 750. The offer is only available until stocks last across all dealerships in India.

BS6 Harley-Davidson Street Rod

BS6 Harley-Davidson Street Rod is the company's second most affordable bike and shares its engine and underpinnings with Street 750. However, the Street Rod is the more premium of the two and this is visible in its styling as well. The bike gets a round headlamp but with a black cowl and a small windscreen, blacked-out mechanical parts.

Harley-Davidson Roadster

If there is one thing that I love about the Harley-Davidson Roadster, it's the way it behaves in moving traffic. The wide handlebar and the steep steering rake give you the leverage to flick the bike around. Tuned to offer a stiff ride, the 43mm inverted front forks respond well to quick direction changes, making the Roadster fun to hustle around the city. Unlike typical cruisers, the Roadster doesn’t feel odd leaning into corners either.
